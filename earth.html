Earth Simulation — Live Data Layers & Physics

A real-time multi-layer Earth simulation combining 20+ live data feeds with physics-based rendering. Every layer — from ocean wave heights to magnetosphere boundaries — is driven by real observations updated every 1–60 minutes. Use the layer panel to toggle any combination, and the time controls to scrub through time.

Surface & Atmosphere
🌍
Earth Surface
Blue Marble base texture with NASA GIBS daily MODIS/VIIRS satellite overlay at 250m resolution. Terrain relief via bump mapping, ocean specular with dual-lobe GGX + sun glitter, polar ice BRDF, and city lights on the nightside. Aerial perspective (Rayleigh in-scattering) adds realistic atmospheric haze at the limb.
☁️
Volumetric Clouds
Full volumetric ray-marched cloud layer with Beer's law absorption, self-shadowing, and Henyey-Greenstein forward scattering. Cloud fractions (low/mid/high) from Open-Meteo GFS at 5° resolution. Cyclonic storm swirl with eye/eyewall structure from live NHC tropical cyclone data.
🌈
Atmosphere Scattering
Physical Rayleigh + Mie scattering with ozone Chappuis band absorption. 24-step ray march with 6-step light rays. Cornette-Shanks phase function for accurate forward scattering. OI 557.7nm green + OI 630nm red + N₂⁺ 391.4nm purple airglow on the nightside. D-layer X-ray blackout glow during solar flares.
🌡️
Weather Data
Live global weather from Open-Meteo / NOAA GFS at 5° grid (72×36 points): temperature, pressure, humidity, wind speed/direction, CAPE. Displayed as temperature colour overlay, isobar contours (marching-squares), wind particle advection, and pressure centre detection (H/L labels). Refreshes every 10 minutes.
🌪️
Storm Alerts
Active severe weather alerts from NOAA NWS displayed as 3D markers with severity-coded colours (yellow/orange/red). Includes tornado warnings, severe thunderstorm warnings, flash flood warnings, winter storms, and tropical cyclone advisories across the continental US.
Magnetosphere & Space Weather
🛡️
Bow Shock & Magnetopause
Shue et al. (1998) magnetopause + Farris-Russell (1994) bow shock, parameterized by live solar wind pressure + IMF Bz from NOAA DSCOVR. Magnetosheath fill, polar cusp funnels, dayside X-line reconnection glow (Bz-driven). Updates every 60 seconds.
🌠
Aurora & Radiation Belts
Multi-arc auroral curtains with altitude-dependent colours (green/red/purple), substorm breakup flickering at Kp > 5. Van Allen belts (inner 1.6 Re, outer 4-6 Re), plasmasphere (Carpenter & Anderson 1992), and ring current Dst injection. All driven by live Kp/Bz/Dst.
💨
Exosphere & Geocorona
Solar-wind-driven exopause shell with Fresnel glow and Alfvénic surface waves. Geocorona hydrogen torus, charge-exchange standoff halo, neutral-H escape plume. Physics from Hodges (1994) and Baliukin (2019) SOHO/SWAN. Nose compressed by solar wind dynamic pressure.
☀️
Solar Wind & Coupling
5,000 solar wind particles deflected by the magnetopause. Akasofu epsilon energy input, Newell merging rate, field-aligned currents (R1+R2), substorm onset index, Alfvén speed. CME transit time estimates from NASA DONKI. L1 propagation delay computed.
NASA Earth Observations (17 layers)
🌊
Ocean Layers
Sea Surface Temperature (JPL MUR SST, daily), Ocean Chlorophyll-a (MODIS ocean colour — phytoplankton blooms), Coral Bleaching Alerts (NOAA CRW 7-day thermal stress), plus NDBC buoy observations — wave height, period, SST, and wind from 1,300 ocean buoys worldwide (10-min refresh via proxy).
🔥
Land & Hazards
Active Fires (MODIS thermal anomalies), Nighttime Lights (VIIRS Day/Night Band — cities, fishing fleets, gas flares), Vegetation NDVI (8-day composite crop/forest health), Land Surface Temperature (MODIS), Snow Cover (MODIS 500m). All from NASA GIBS, daily updates.
🌫️
Atmosphere Observations
Precipitation Rate (GPM IMERG, 30-min near-real-time), Aerosol Optical Depth (MODIS AOD — dust, smoke, pollution), Ozone Column (OMPS — ozone hole tracking), Dust/Aerosol Index (OMPS UV), Water Vapor (AIRS — atmospheric rivers), Lightning (GOES GLM flash density). Cloud optical thickness for storm intensity.
❄️
Cryosphere
Sea Ice Concentration (AMSR2 12km — Arctic + Antarctic daily extent), Snow Cover (MODIS binary classification at 500m). Tracks seasonal ice growth and retreat across both polar regions. Climate monitoring visualization.
Geophysical Events & Satellite Tracking
🌋
Earthquakes & Geohazards
Live USGS earthquake feed — M2.5+ events from the past 30 days with magnitude-scaled 3D markers (InstancedMesh for one draw call). Color-coded by magnitude (blue M2.5 → red M7+). Tectonic plate boundaries from USGS PB2002 GeoJSON. Volcanic eruption alerts from USGS VHP, tsunami warnings from NOAA NWS.
🛰️
Satellite Tracking
SGP4 orbit propagation (WASM + JS fallback) from CelesTrak TLEs. Track ISS, Starlink (5,000+), weather satellites, GPS/GNSS, and debris. Conjunction screening with 72-hour look-ahead. Metadata enrichment from Space-Track.org (object type, country, RCS size) via server proxy.
🌬️
Air Quality & Jet Stream
Global PM2.5 air quality from OpenAQ (80,000+ monitoring stations with EPA AQI calculation). Jet stream position from NOAA GFS 250hPa wind at 2° resolution via NOMADS proxy. Wind speeds >30 m/s (108 km/h) visualized as the jet stream core.
