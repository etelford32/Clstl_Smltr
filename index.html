Why Parkers Physics

Built on real physics. Run on real data.

Most space visualizations are pre-rendered or decorative. Ours compute the physics equations live and pull actual satellite telemetry — the same data NASA mission operators use.

📡

Live Satellite Data

Geomagnetic storm indices, solar wind velocity, IMF Bz, X-ray flux, and CME arrival times update continuously from NOAA SWPC, NASA DONKI, and GOES satellites. Storm mode escalates refresh rates to 20 seconds when Kp ≥ 6 or an X-class flare fires.

⚛️

Real Physics Engines

Simulations implement published equations: Parker spiral solar wind, Shue et al. (1998) magnetopause, Rankine-Hugoniot bow shock, VSOP87 planetary ephemeris, and McPherron (1995) substorm index — not artistic approximations.

🎮

3D WebGL Rendering

Three.js with custom GLSL shaders, HDR bloom post-processing, and GPU-accelerated particle systems. Up to 50,000 particles per scene. Runs entirely in your browser — no downloads, no installs, no plugins.
