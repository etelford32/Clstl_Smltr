Who Uses Parker Physics

Built for anyone who needs to understand space weather

From mission control rooms to university classrooms — the same live data, the same physics engines.

🔬

Researchers & Scientists

Space physicists, heliophysicists, and magnetosphere researchers use live NOAA/NASA feeds to correlate observations with model predictions. Export CDM, CSV, and JSON for downstream analysis pipelines. AR(6) Kp forecast models with 80% confidence intervals.

🎓

Educators & Students

University STEM courses use our interactive 3D simulations to teach orbital mechanics, plasma physics, and stellar evolution. Students see real solar flares happen in real-time instead of reading about them in textbooks. Free tier covers all core physics.

🛰️

Satellite Operators

Track 30,000+ objects with SGP4 propagation, predict conjunctions 72 hours ahead, and generate CDM reports. Radiation environment analysis for mission planning. ISS pass predictions for your ground station. Debris field visualization for situational awareness.

📻

HAM Radio & Communications

HF propagation depends on ionospheric conditions driven by solar X-ray flux and geomagnetic storms. Our D-layer blackout visualization and Kp forecast show when HF radio will be disrupted — critical for emergency communications and DX operations.

🌌

Aurora Chasers

See aurora probability forecasts for your exact location. 72-hour Kp predictions with storm probability percentages. Push alerts when Kp exceeds your visibility threshold. Know exactly when and where to look — no more guessing from generic forecasts.

⚡

Power Grid & Aviation

Geomagnetically induced currents (GIC) threaten power infrastructure during G4-G5 storms. Our real-time Dst monitoring and storm probability models give grid operators advance warning. Aviation professionals track radiation dose rates and HF blackout zones.

Why Parker Physics

Built on real physics. Run on real data.

Most space visualizations are pre-rendered or decorative. Ours compute the physics equations live and pull actual satellite telemetry — the same data NASA mission operators use.

📡

Live Satellite Data

Geomagnetic storm indices, solar wind velocity, IMF Bz, X-ray flux, and CME arrival times update continuously from NOAA SWPC, NASA DONKI, and GOES satellites. Storm mode escalates refresh rates to 20 seconds when Kp ≥ 6 or an X-class flare fires.

⚛️

Real Physics Engines

Simulations implement published equations: Parker spiral solar wind, Shue et al. (1998) magnetopause, Rankine-Hugoniot bow shock, VSOP87 planetary ephemeris, and McPherron (1995) substorm index — not artistic approximations.

🎮

3D WebGL Rendering

Three.js with custom GLSL shaders, HDR bloom post-processing, and GPU-accelerated particle systems. Up to 50,000 particles per scene. Runs entirely in your browser — no downloads, no installs, no plugins.
