References & Accuracy

Limitations: TLEs are mean elements — SGP4 is the ONLY correct propagator. Position accuracy ~1 km at epoch, degrades ~1-2 km/day for LEO. Conjunction screening uses simplified J2 propagation; production CDM generation requires full SGP4 for both target AND catalog with proper covariance estimation.
