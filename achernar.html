Physics Notes▼

Roche figure: surface = isopotential of Φgrav+Φcent. Solved iteratively per-vertex: r = 1 + (β/2)·r³·sin²θ in polar units. Surface normal taken from −∇Φ.

von Zeipel gravity darkening: T(θ) = Tpole·(g(θ)/gpole)β with β=0.25 for radiative envelopes (B-stars). Equator emits at <60% of polar Teff.

Planck colour: per-vertex T mapped to RGB via Tanner-Helland blackbody fit — not an A-star piecewise approximation.

Be decretion disk: ionized H plasma ~10⁴ K in Keplerian rotation, ρ ∝ r−3.5, emitting Hα (656 nm). Variable — Achernar episodically sheds & reforms it.
